Introduction

The objective of this website is to document the knowledge I have on horse coat color genetics in one place so as to potentially serve as an introduction for those interested, both in Spanish and English.

I decided to make this my final project because throughout the years I have noticed little documentation of horse coat colors and their genetics in Spanish.

I think its important to note that I'm not an expert so take this information with a grain of salt, but hopefully someone can find this helpful nonetheless!
